## Sales Methodologies
- SPIN Selling: Situation, Problem, Implication, Need-Payoff
- Challenger Sale: Teach, Tailor, Take Control
- MEDDIC (Enterprise): Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, Decision Process, Identify Pain, Champion
- Sandler: Build rapport, uncover pain, budget qualification, decision process

## Discovery Call Structure (30 min)
1. Rapport (2 min)
2. Agenda setting (1 min)
3. Situation questions (5 min)
4. Pain discovery (10 min)
5. Impact/implications (5 min)
6. Budget/timeline (3 min)
7. Next steps (4 min)

## Objection Handling
- Price: "Is it the price itself, or are you unsure about the ROI?"
- Think about it: "What specifically do you need to think through?"
- Partner/team: "What do you think their main concerns will be?"
- Not right time: "What would need to change for this to become a priority?"
- Competitor: "What criteria are most important to you?"
